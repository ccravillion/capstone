What to do Next

So you have your invention all planned and ready to make, what happens now? This is where The Invention Group comes into play. We are able to help you with your invention by providing constructive feedback as well as providing resources to use for improvements and additions. We always want to help where we can when it comes to creative minds looking to grow and get something meaningful out of their work.


How the Process Works

In order to gain help and feedback on your invention, it will need to be implemented into our system. This includes what your invention is, what it does, and the reason for its creation. Other elements will need to be submitted that are associated with your invention. This can include prototypes, files of your invention, schematics, etc. The Create page on our website is the place you will need to go to in order to add your invention to our system. There you will find a form to fill in with all of your invention's information. Once the form is submitted, We will notify you that your information has been recieved and reviewed. Others will be able to view your invention and use it as inspiration as well as provide their own inputs and feedback. From then on, you will have access to our resources and infrastructure to continue making your creations a reality.
